Navy Anchorage School and College Dhaka

Navy Anchorage School and College Dhaka (NASCD) is an English Version institution patronized by the Bangladesh Navy. This institution started its journey on 01 January 2000 at Naval Headquarters (Banani) as an English Medium School with the motto Learning For Life. In 2012, it was shifted to its new campus (Khilkhet Naval Area) under the National Curriculum.With the passage of time, it has been upgraded to the college level and earned the position of being one of the most prestigious institutions in Bangladesh. It is a unique institution with its beautiful campus, qualified faculty members, and congenial atmosphere provided for knowledge seekers.My school has many clubs and activities. it has two buildings, one for kinder garden and primary. The other one is for secondary and college. I am a proud student of this institution.
